About Us

The future of EDM is here. Where independent artists thrive.

Our Story

ZoundZ Nation was born from a simple idea: independent artists deserve better. In a world where major labels take massive cuts and gatekeep opportunities, we decided to do things differently. We're a collective of music enthusiasts and industry professionals who believe in the power of EDM and the artists who create it.

Our Mission

We're on a mission to democratize music distribution. Our goal is to put your music on the global stage without the gatekeeping. High-quality distribution, professional mastering feedback, and pricing that actually makes sense for independent artists — that's what we're all about.

What We Offer

  • Fast Distribution — Get your tracks on Spotify, Apple Music, and TikTok in record time
  • Artist First Pricing — Our pricing model is built for artists, not corporations
  • Pro Quality Feedback — Access to professional mastering guidance
  • Community Support — Join a network of like-minded EDM producers

Why EDM?

EDM isn't just a genre — it's a movement. The infectious basslines, the funky grooves, the energy that makes you want to move. We specialize in EDM because we believe it's the sound that connects people across cultures and backgrounds. It's music that makes you feel alive.
